# United Nations General Debate

Every year at the United Nations, member states deliver statements during the General Debate discussing major issues in world politics. These speeches provide invaluable information on governments' perspectives and preferences on a wide range of issues. The United Nations General Debate Corpus (UNGDC) covers the period 1970-2017 and contains 7,897 speeches by the heads of state and government or their representatives.
